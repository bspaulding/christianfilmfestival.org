Real life testimonies of individuals who came forward making commitments to Christ at the front of the theater as recorded by Christian Film Festival counselors.

13th Annual Merrimack Valley Christian Film Festival

A 12 year old girl who had a rare crippling disease in which many of her joints were locked attended on Tuesday evening. From what I understand she might not have too much longer to live. On earth that is, because after watching Sting: Moment of Truth, it was the “day of her Salvation” as she gave her life to Jesus Christ. After counseling was finished we brought her to meet Sting in person. He took time to share with her and encourage her. Then this big hulk of a man picked up this tiny crippled girl and carried her in his arms through the lobby to the exit. It was a very emotional moving experience for everyone as many tears were being shed.

2nd Annual Upstate South Carolina Christian Film Festival

Payton and his family came to the Regal Cinemas  Saturday morning to see a secular movie.   When they saw the free Christian  movies that were being shown they decided to go to the Children’s Matinee. Payton came forward at the invitation and gave his life to Jesus.

A Chinese American couple came forward to commit their lives to Jesus Christ at a Children’s matinee! The husband was 59 years old and his wife is 57 years old.  The movie that God used to speak to their hearts was Ben Hur-A Tale of Christ.

11th Annual Merrimack Valley Christian Film Festival

Throughout the week a number of people who were members of the Boston Church of Christ cult came forward to receive Jesus as their personal Lord and Savior.  All were referred to Evangelical / Bible believing churches for follow-up.

Three sisters ages 12, 14, & 15 who moved to So. Lawrence a few months ago from Philadelphia were eating at Taco Bell on Rt. 114.  A woman was walking around giving out free tickets to the Christian Film Festival.  She gave tickets to the 3 girls.  They came to the film festival and when the invitation was given all three responded together.  They came forward and received Jesus as their Lord and Savior.  They had no church affiliation and never clearly understood who Jesus was until today!  They came back the next day and went forward for special prayer.  The day before they didn’t really know what prayer was.

3rd Annual Greater Orlando Christian Film Festival

We have been able to participate in the Christian film festival for the 3rd time now. Each year we have had at least one teen from our youth group come and accept Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord – this year was no exception. We had 2 teenage boys in high school, 1 teen girl, and a youth worker’s 8-year old son all come to Christ tonight. Praise the Lord! We have been praying for this for months, and God has answered beyond our hopes in a “4” for one night.
