Read Our Mission Statement & Statement of Purpose

Christian Film Festivals of America, Inc., is a non-profit corporation. The Christian Film Festival ministry travels to different cities throughout the US, rents 2 screens in a large cinema complex for 1 week, and presents ten to fourteen different Christian movies from approximately 11 AM to 11 PM continuously for the seven or eight days and nights. There are approximately 75 - 85 shows during that time period.

The one and only purpose of the Christian Film Festival is evangelism with the ultimate goal of leading people to a saving knowledge of Jesus Christ. A live invitation is given at the end of every show. When people come forward there are trained counselors at the front of the theater to share and pray with those who respond to the invitation. All those making commitments to Christ are referred back to only Bible believing / evangelical churches for follow-up.

37 film festivals have been held around the country; 18 in New England including the latest Greater Boston Christian Film Festival, 4 on the central coast of California, 2 in Southern California, 6 in Orlando, Florida, 3 in the Upstate area of South Carolina, 2 in Syracuse, NY and 1 each in Memphis TN and Rochester, NY. Attendance has been just under 364,000. There have been close to 22,000 commitments to Christ made at these film festivals.

Admission is FREE to each and every show and no offering is taken in the theater so that the unbelievers who attend do not feel pressured or intimidated in any way.
